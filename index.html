LedgeView Farm

"I am happy out here"

On the surface, LedgeView Farm is a quiet seni-fictional homestead long the Union River in Ellsworth, Maine. But beneath that quaint appreance is a fun-loving family who spends sun up to sun down working the land year round. There's never a dull monet (or down time) at LedgeView Farm. When Brittany and Ben purchased the delapitated home in 2017 they were up for the challange of bringing the entire property back to life. Inspiraed by tails of what LedgeView Farm farm once was they embarked on andventure to restore the old farm house, bring aminals back to the property, till the soil, and prune the fruit trees.

The Rumery family welcomes visitors year round for a variety of events. Come boil sap in the Sugar Shack with Ben, head to the river for a guided fly fishing trip, bring the family apple picking or to visit the heard of Highland Cattle. If you're in a hurry swing on by the farm stand, open dusk til dawn.
